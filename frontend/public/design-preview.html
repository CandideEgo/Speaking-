It's the most powerful sound in the world.
它是世界上最强大的声音。
It's the only one that can start a war or say "I love you."
唯一能开启一场战争,也能说"我爱你"的声音。
And yet many people find that when they speak, people don't listen.
然而很多人发现,当他们说话时,人们并不听。
This is a serious problem that we can fix.
这是个我们可以解决的严重问题。
I'd like to suggest there are things we can do to change that.
我想建议,我们可以做些事情来改变它。
Seven deadly sins of speaking — let me list them.
说话的七宗罪——让我列出来。
